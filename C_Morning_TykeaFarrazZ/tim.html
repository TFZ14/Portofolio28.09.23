Farmlace

[Full-Stack Development] Farmlace Web Application was developed to facilitate the check-in/check-out process for Field Extension Officers or PPL (Petugas Penyuluh Lapangan), during their inspections of partner farms (Mitra). The main idea behind creating this application is for the company to collaborate with Mitra, and require PPLs to conduct regular inspections on their behalf. The Check-in and Check-out of PPLs will be recorded in the database as attendance reports. Additionally, FEOs can make reports base on condition of Mitra's farms to the company through this web application.
